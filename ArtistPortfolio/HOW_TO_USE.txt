# Artist Portfolio Creator - Demo Guide

**What is this?**
This app builds a professional artist portfolio website in seconds using AI. It writes your bio, categorizes your art, and designs the layout automatically.

**How to use:**
1.  **Open the Link:** Go to the provided URL.
2.  **Enter Details:** Type your name (or a persona name) and a short description of your art style (e.g., "Abstract expressionism with a focus on urban decay").
3.  **Upload or Generate:**
    *   **Upload:** If you have art files, upload them.
    *   **Generate:** If not, check "Use AI Placeholders" to let the AI create sample art for you.
4.  **Create:** Click the button and watch the site build itself.
5.  **Explore:** Scroll through your new portfolio. The "About Me" and "Artist Statement" are all written by AI based on your short description.

**Note:** This is a demo. The portfolio is generated instantly in your browser.
